Why This Matters

Consortiums Have Outsized Power — When They Have the Right Legal Support

Individual small businesses face steep barriers: navigating complex certification processes, decoding government RFPs, challenging unfair procurement decisions, and staying compliant with changing regulations.

Business associations, chambers of commerce, and advocacy groups can change that equation. With the right organizational structure, legal standing, and advocacy strategy, a consortium can open doors for dozens or hundreds of businesses at once.

Rosales Law Partners provides the legal expertise that makes that possible — from organizational governance and nonprofit compliance to DBE program engagement and policy advocacy at the local, state, and federal level.

How We Help Consortiums & Advocacy Groups

From organizational formation to active policy advocacy, we provide legal counsel built around the specific needs of groups that represent small businesses.

🏢

Organizational Formation & Governance

Whether you are forming a new advocacy organization, restructuring an existing one, or need a governance review, we advise on entity selection, bylaws, operating agreements, board responsibilities, and nonprofit compliance requirements.

📢

DBE Program Advocacy & Engagement

We help consortiums participate in government processes that shape DBE and SBE programs — disparity studies, goal-setting hearings, public comment periods, and formal stakeholder engagement with transit authorities and public agencies.

🏅

Member Certification Support

We support consortiums in guiding members through DBE, SBE, LBE, and ACDBE certification applications, helping members respond to agency requests for information, and representing members in certification appeals when applications are denied.

📋

Contract & Partnership Agreements

From member agreements to formal partnerships with government agencies and prime contractors, we draft and review contracts that protect your organization and create durable, legally sound relationships.

⚖️

Procurement Policy & Regulatory Advocacy

We advise on regulatory comment strategy, formal advocacy before state and federal agencies, and the legal frameworks that govern small business participation goals in public contracting — equipping your organization to advocate with authority.

🎓

Member Education & Legal Clinics

We partner with business associations to deliver workshops, legal clinics, and educational resources on government contracting, certification, and business legal fundamentals — directly benefiting your membership base.
