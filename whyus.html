Why Small Business Owners Choose Us

We are not just advocates for small businesses — we are one. Certified, experienced, and committed to helping your business grow.

Passion, Experience, and a Shared Mission

Rosales Law Partners LLP was founded in 2009 with one mission: to level the playing field for small, minority, and women-owned businesses in government contracting and public law.

We offer comprehensive legal services — transactional work, litigation, special counsel, and general counsel — for private and governmental clients of all sizes, across the country.

What sets us apart is that we treat every client, attorney, and staff member as a collaborative partner. Your success is our success.
